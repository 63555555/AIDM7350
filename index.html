The history of Disney

Disney, let your soul feel true happiness

  • Dream makers

    Walt Disney Disney was born in 1901 in Illinois, in the Midwest of the United States, and grew up on a farm. He has been interested in painting since he was a child, and after the First World War, he began working on Alice in Wonderland.

  • The beginning of a dream

    After the Alice series, Walt and animator Ub Iwerk created the first animated cartoon character with a character's personality in 1927, which was the first popular animated character he designed and the predecessor of Mickey's character.

  • Huge change

    In 1928, when Walter went to New York to renew his contract with the producer, the negotiations did not go well, and not only did he lose his rights, but most of the studio's animators were poached. This forced him to start over.

  • Gradually, it is improving

    In 1937, Disney released their first full-length animated feature, Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs. It was a huge success and became the highest-grossing film of its time.

  • Triumphant

    Disney has left an indelible mark on the entertainment industry. Its influence and magic continue to inspire and captivate people of all ages across the globe.
